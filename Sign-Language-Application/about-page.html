About SignaChat

Breaking communication barriers and making the world more accessible through innovative sign language technology.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

SignaChat is dedicated to breaking communication barriers between sign language users and the hearing world. We believe that technology should empower everyone to communicate freely, regardless of language differences. Our mission is to create innovative solutions that make real-time, bi-directional translation between sign language and spoken/written language accessible to all.

Our Vision

We envision a world where communication barriers no longer exist, where technology serves as a bridge between different forms of language, and where accessibility is not an afterthought but a fundamental right. Through our cutting-edge applications and services, we aim to foster a more inclusive society where everyone can participate equally in all aspects of life.

Who We Serve

For Sign Language Users

  • Communicate by signing to a camera with the conversation converted into text
  • Learn English through interactive translation tools
  • Access educational content with sign language translations

For Non-Sign Language Users

  • Learn sign language through interactive lessons and tutorials
  • Communicate with sign language users using our translation tools
  • Access a comprehensive sign language dictionary with video demonstrations

For Accessibility

  • Overlay an AR version of the avatar during video calls or on recorded lectures
  • Access educational content with both spoken and sign language translations

Core Features

🔄

Real-Time Translation

Our Audio-to-Sign feature captures audio from your device's microphone, converts it to text using advanced speech recognition services, processes the text using NLP, and animates our 3D avatar to display sign language gestures in real-time.

📝

Sign-to-Text Conversion

Using your device's camera, our technology captures sign language gestures, processes them through computer vision and machine learning algorithms, and converts them into accurate text that can be further processed or shared.

🔊

Text-to-Sign Animation

Type text and have it translated into sign language through our animated 3D avatar, making communication more accessible for sign language users.

📱

AR Avatar & Video Overlay

Record class slides along with audio and overlay an AR version of our 3D avatar that translates the spoken content into sign language, enhancing accessibility in educational settings.

📚

Interactive Learning Modules

Whether you're a sign language user learning English or someone wanting to learn sign language, our interactive lessons, vocabulary exercises, and quizzes make learning engaging and effective.

🔍

Sign Language Dictionary

Access our comprehensive searchable database with video examples of sign language gestures, making it easier for users to look up and learn specific signs.

Our Technology

SignBridge leverages cutting-edge technologies across multiple domains to deliver a seamless translation experience. Our architecture combines advanced AI, computer vision, 3D animation, and augmented reality to create a powerful platform.

Front End Technologies

Web

  • React for user interface development
  • Three.js/Babylon.js for 3D rendering of avatars
  • WebXR for augmented reality experiences
  • MediaStream API for camera and microphone capture

Mobile

  • React Native or Flutter with native camera support
  • ARCore for Android devices
  • ARKit for iOS devices

Back End Technologies

Server Framework

  • Node.js (Express) or Python (FastAPI/Django)

Speech & Sign Processing

  • Google Cloud Speech-to-Text for audio transcription
  • NLP models for text-to-sign mapping
  • TensorFlow/PyTorch for sign language recognition
  • FFmpeg/OpenCV for video processing and AR overlay

Data Storage

  • MongoDB or PostgreSQL for user data and content
  • Cloud storage for educational videos and animations
